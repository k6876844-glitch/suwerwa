School Policies

The framework that keeps MHSK a fair, safe, and excellent place to learn, live, and grow.

Commitment Fairness Integrity

Our policies exist to safeguard the values that define Moi High School Kabarak: commitment, fairness, and integrity. They establish a clear, consistent framework within which every student can flourish academically, socially, and spiritually and every member of staff can do their best work.

Each policy listed below reflects our pursuit of excellence within a Christian, learner-centered environment. Policies are reviewed regularly by the school management and Board to ensure they remain current and aligned with the school's mission.

Policy Framework

Academic Policy

Standards for teaching, learning, assessment, and academic integrity across the school.

Education

Code of Conduct

Expected behaviour for all students, both on and off campus, in line with our Christian ethos.

Discipline

Boarding & Welfare

Rules and procedures governing dormitory life, student welfare, and house leadership.

Boarding

School Uniform Policy

Approved attire for all students, including sports kit and Sunday-best dress code.

Uniform

ICT & Mobile Devices

Acceptable use of technology, internet access, and personal electronics.

Technology

Safeguarding & Child Protection

Comprehensive safeguarding framework to protect every student's wellbeing.

Safety

Health & Medical Policy

Sanatorium procedures, medication handling, and emergency response protocols.

Health

Library & Resources

Borrowing rules, study expectations, and care of school learning materials.

Library

Chapel & Religious Life

Worship attendance, religious instruction, and fostering Christian values.

Faith

Anti-Bullying Policy

Zero-tolerance framework for bullying, harassment, and discrimination of any kind.

Wellbeing

Guiding Principles

  • Every policy is anchored in our Christian foundation and mission
  • Fairness and consistency apply equally to every student and staff member
  • Parents and guardians are partners in upholding the school's standards
  • Policies are reviewed regularly by the Board of Governors
  • Detailed policy documents are available from the school administration office on request

For detailed policy documents, please contact the school administration office or refer to the Student/Parent Handbook issued at admission.
