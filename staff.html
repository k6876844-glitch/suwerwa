Our Profile

A top-performing co-educational private secondary school rooted in Christian values, academic rigour, and holistic student development.

MHS
K

Motto: "ON EARTH WE RISE!"

Moi High School Kabarak is a top-performing school located in Nakuru County, Kenya. The School is well-known for its academic and co-curricular excellence, offering a vibrant, stimulating learning environment that prioritizes the holistic development of students.

MHSK provides a rigorous academic program that prepares students for success in higher education and beyond. The School has a highly qualified teaching staff who use modern teaching techniques to make learning more engaging, interactive and memorable. Whether in mathematics, science, humanities, or languages, the school's curriculum is designed to challenge students and aim for intellectual growth.

The school's emphasis on co-curricular activities enables students to develop skills and talents that complement their academic studies. With a well-established library, well-equipped classrooms, and modern facilities, students at Moi High School Kabarak are assured of quality learning experiences.

The school offers a diverse and supportive environment that encourages students to develop into well-rounded individuals. With active clubs and societies, peer mentoring, and counseling services on campus, MHSK creates a supportive community that fosters lifelong learning. The School has a rich culture that values respect, discipline, and responsibility.

We have a long-standing tradition of producing notable alumni who have made significant contributions to society. The school's alumni are highly respected in fields such as medicine, politics, business, and education. Studying at Moi High School Kabarak opens doors to extensive opportunities for networking and mentorship.

What Sets Us Apart

Academic Excellence

Consistent top KCSE performance driven by qualified staff and modern teaching.

Christian Foundation

All endeavors anchored in Christian values, integrity, and fear of the Lord.

Holistic Growth

Sports, clubs, leadership, and arts complement classroom learning.

Curriculum

In the first two years, students take a rich blend of compulsory and optional subjects designed to build a solid academic foundation. From Form Three, students sit a minimum of 7 subjects in the KCSE examination as per KNEC guidelines most students opt for 8.

Compulsory Subjects

  • Mathematics
  • English
  • Kiswahili
  • Biology
  • Chemistry
  • Physics
  • Geography
  • History
  • Christian Religious Education

Optional Subjects (choose one)

  • Agriculture
  • Business Studies
  • French
  • Computer Studies
  • German
  • Music
KCSE Examination: From Form 3 onwards, students are examined on a minimum of 7 subjects and a maximum of 8 subjects as per Kenya National Examinations Council (KNEC) guidelines.

Our Location

Moi High School Kabarak is situated 20 km from Nakuru town in Kenya's Rift Valley. The school shares its beautiful compound with Kabarak University, creating a unique academic ecosystem that inspires students toward higher education.
