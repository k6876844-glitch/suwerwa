Notable Alumni

From politics and law to academia and journalism meet the MHSK graduates who are shaping Kenya and the world.

Once a Kabarakian, Always a Kabarakian

"Generations of the youth will always be grateful..."

You won't believe the number of notable alumni that Moi High School Kabarak has produced over the years from top political figures and renowned journalists to successful entrepreneurs and high-profile sports personalities. With such a diverse range of accomplished individuals emerging from MHSK, it's no wonder our school continues to be one of the most respected educational institutions in the country.
