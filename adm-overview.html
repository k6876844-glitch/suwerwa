Admission Overview

Begin your journey at one of Kenya's most respected schools — where academic rigor meets holistic development.

★

Excellence · Discipline · Faith

★
🎓

"Admitting tomorrow's leaders, today."

Moi High School Kabarak is one of the leading schools in Kenya — known for academic excellence, social development, and all-around growth. We admit students from across the country and abroad, operating as a national school under the Kenyan Education system.

It is worth noting that admission to Moi High School Kabarak is not solely based on academic performance. The school encourages and supports students' all-around development, and therefore considers other factors such as co-curricular activities, community service, and leadership skills.

MHSK is dedicated to offering quality education and a conducive learning environment to its students. The school's admission process is carefully designed to ensure that only the most deserving students join the MHSK family — and this commitment to selection has contributed to the school's excellent academic performance and outstanding reputation over the years.

What Guides Our Admission

📚

Academic Excellence

Strong academic foundation and the ability to thrive in a rigorous, challenging curriculum.

✝️

Christian Values

Alignment with our Christian ethos and willingness to participate in spiritual life.

🌟

Holistic Potential

Demonstrated growth in sports, arts, leadership, and community engagement.

🔍 Beyond the Grade Book

Our admissions panel evaluates the whole student. Here's what counts alongside academic performance:

✓ Co-curricular involvement
✓ Community service record
✓ Leadership experience
✓ Character & integrity
✓ Sports & athletics
✓ Arts, music & drama
