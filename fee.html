Fee Payment

Transparent fee structure with flexible payment options designed to support every MHSK family.

★

Affordable · Flexible · Transparent

★

Transparent. Predictable. Manageable.

The school's fee structure is comprehensive and covers all expenses for students across all classes. We are committed to providing clarity and flexibility so that no qualified student misses out on a quality education.

Fee Categories

🎯

Admission Fee

A one-time, non-refundable fee charged to new students joining MHSK. Covers registration, induction materials, and initial onboarding.

New Students Only
📚

Tuition Fees

Covers academic instruction, learning materials, examinations, and access to the library and laboratories. Tuition varies depending on the class level.

Billed Termly
🏠

Boarding Fees

Covers accommodation in our well-maintained dormitories along with three nutritious meals per day. Boarding fees vary by class level.

Termly Boarders

💳 Approved Payment Methods

🏦

Bank Deposit

Direct deposit to the school's official bank account using the parent's bank slip.

Account details provided with admission letter
📱

M-Pesa Paybill

Convenient mobile money transfer using the school's official Paybill number.

Paybill: See admission documents
💻

Electronic Transfer

Online bank-to-bank transfer (EFT/RTGS) for institutional and corporate payments.

SWIFT details available on request
🏢

Bankers' Cheque

Payment by bankers' cheque addressed to Moi High School Kabarak.

Submit to the school finance office

🤝 Our Flexible Fee Plan

The management of Moi High School Kabarak operates a comprehensive fee payment plan that enables parents and guardians to fund the school fees comfortably — without undue strain or disruption to learning. Students' education continuity is our priority.

Termly instalment options
Multiple payment channels
Transparent fee breakdown
Receipt for every payment
Continuity-first approach
Confidential consultation
