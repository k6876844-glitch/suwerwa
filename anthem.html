The song that captures the soul of Moi High School Kabarak a hymn of faith, ambition, and gratitude.

On Earth We Rise

School Anthem

On Earth We Rise

The voice of Moi High School Kabarak sung at assemblies, celebrations, and ceremonies as a reminder of who we are and where we're going.

Verse 1
On Earth we rise, like a city on the mountain O yes, like a city on a mountain Taking up the challenge being seen across the world O yes, being seen right across the world We aim at the sun and we know we'll reach the top O yes, we know we'll reach the top And with God on our side, we know the sky is the limit O yes, the sky is the limit Now let us rise, and shine Let's work even harder than now Yes, let us rise and shine We are the best cause we trust in the Lord
Verse 2
The Christian faith that holds us together O yes, it holds us together Teaching us good morals within Christianity O yes, within Christianity Invigorates when weary, and sanctions our happy times O yes, it sanctions our happy times Lays foundations of life, and gives us hope on earth O yes, it gives us hope on earth
Verse 3
We thank the Lord, for the Founder of Kabarak O yes, for the Founder of Kabarak Taking the decision for the benefit of Kenyan youths O yes, for the benefit of Kenyan youths Generations of the youth will always be grateful O yes, they will always be grateful We pray that Kabarak be a burning spear forever O yes, be a burning spear Forever.
Composed by
David Zalo Okuku
