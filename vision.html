Mission, Vision & Values

The principles that shape every student, every classroom, every choice at MHSK.

Vision Mission Values

Guiding Principles

At Moi High School Kabarak, every decision we make and every student we shape is anchored in three guiding lights: a clear Vision, a purposeful Mission, and unwavering Core Values. Together they define who we are, what we do, and why we do it.

Our Vision

To be a Christian School of Choice the institution that families, communities, and the nation trust to nurture the next generation.

Our Mission

To provide holistic educational services in a learner-centered, co-educational Christian set-up, guided by the values of commitment, fairness, and integrity remaining accountable to the maxim of excellence in all our undertakings.

Core Values

The principles we live by, daily, on the playground and in the classroom:

Commitment Fairness Integrity

Living Our Values

Commitment means showing up for our students, for our standards, for our faith. From the chalkboard to the chapel, we don't do anything halfway.

Fairness guides how we discipline, how we grade, how we choose leaders, and how we treat every member of the MHSK family regardless of background or status.

Integrity is the silent partner in every transaction at Kabarak. It's what our students carry out into the world long after they leave our gates.

"In fulfilling our mission, we remain accountable to the maxim of excellence in all our undertakings."
