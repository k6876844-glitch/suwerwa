School Administration

Distinguished leadership guiding MHSK with vision, integrity, and a commitment to excellence.

Leadership Built on Trust & Service

The administration of Moi High School Kabarak is built on a foundation of distinguished leadership, blending visionary governance with hands-on academic stewardship. From our Board of Directors and Trustees to the day-to-day Management, every leader plays a role in fulfilling our mission of holistic, Christian education.
