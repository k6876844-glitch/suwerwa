How To Apply

Six clear steps from filling the form to receiving your admission letter — start your MHSK journey today.

★

Apply · Test · Interview · Enrol

★

"Begin your MHSK journey today."

Moi High School Kabarak is one of the most prestigious schools in Kenya, with a reputation for academic excellence and the holistic development of its students. Below are the comprehensive guidelines on how to apply for admission.

Step-by-Step Application Process

1

📋Obtain the Application Form

The first step is to obtain the official application form. You may collect it directly from Moi High School Kabarak, or download it from the school's website. Complete the form accurately and legibly, providing all required information including bio-data, academic records, and personal information.

📥 Available Online or On-Site
2

💳Pay the Application Fee

Upon completing the application form, candidates must pay a non-refundable application fee. Payment can be made via bank deposit or electronic payment. The bank account details and full payment options will be provided together with the application form.

💰 Non-refundable Fee
3

📤Submit Your Application

Submit the completed application form, academic records, and supporting documents to the school's admissions office.

Supporting Documents Required:
Completed application form
Certified copy of birth certificate
Recommendation letter from current school
Two passport-size photographs
4

📝Sit the Entrance Examination

After submission, candidates will be required to sit for an entrance examination — mandatory for all applicants. The exam assesses academic ability and suitability for MHSK's rigorous curriculum.

Exam Subjects:
📐Mathematics
📖English
🔬General Science
5

🎤Attend the Interview

Candidates who perform well in the entrance examination will be invited for an interview, typically conducted by a panel of school officials. The interview assesses interpersonal skills, leadership potential, and other non-academic attributes that are critical for success at Moi High School Kabarak.

👥 Panel Interview
6

🎉Receive Your Admission Decision

Finally, successful candidates will be notified of their admission status and offered a place at Moi High School Kabarak. The admission letter will include the fee structure, reporting date, and details on what to bring on the first day.

🏆 Welcome to MHSK!

✅ Pre-Application Checklist

Make sure you have these ready before starting your application:

✓ Birth certificate (certified copy)
✓ Recent academic records
✓ School recommendation letter
✓ Two passport-size photographs
✓ Application fee payment
✓ Parent/Guardian contact details
