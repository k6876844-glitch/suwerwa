Who We Are

Welcome to Moi High School Kabarak

Moi High School Kabarak is a top-performing school located in Nakuru County, Kenya. The school is well-known for its academic and co-curricular excellence, offering a vibrant, stimulating learning environment that prioritises the holistic development of students.

The school provides a rigorous academic programme that prepares students for success in higher education and beyond, with a highly qualified teaching staff who use modern techniques to make learning engaging, interactive, and memorable.

With a well-established library, well-equipped classrooms, and modern facilities, students at MHS-Kabarak are assured of quality learning experiences.

Embodiment of Hope

Moi High School Kabarak anchors all its endeavors on the fear of the Lord and Christian values. The Board of Directors, the school administration, the staff, students and all stakeholders put God first in their day-to-day activities.
