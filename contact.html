Find Us

Our Location

St Francis Girls Secondary School – Suwerwa, Trans Nzoia County, Kenya

How to Find Us

  • From Kitale Town: Take Suwerwa Road heading north, approximately 8 km from Kitale Town centre.
  • By Matatu: Board Kitale–Suwerwa matatus from Kitale main stage. Alight at Suwerwa junction.
  • On Foot: A 5-minute walk from Suwerwa junction following the school signboards.
